Bachelor of Engineering - Information Technology


The programme is designed to make a strong foundation in IT that includes electronics and hardware. Information Technology courses are introduced in the early semesters while non-IT courses in the later stages. The courses in mathematics, sciences, engineering and humanities enable students to explore the wider applications of technology.
